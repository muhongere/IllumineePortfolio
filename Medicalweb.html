Four pages of Medical Management

Pages on the website will include the home page, where a nurse, physician or other user needs to login in with their credentials, along with navigation to subpages for materials, patient records and support. On the home page, it will outline all the services that are provided by the website. There will be a navigation bar for easy access to these 3 main services of the website. Users can navigate to different pages by just clicking on of the links in the navigation bar. The 2nd and 3rd tier will provide the user with the main info they are looking for. There are several elements that will be included in tier 3 pages; for example, patient records page will include info on patient assessments, medication orders, and treatment orders. Under patient records users can also input assessments, admissions and discharges for patients, vitals sign, MRA (medication administration related), output and input, follow-up of falls and treatments and other important patient info. The support pages will have a place where support contact info is provided and a page where tickets can be submitted to support for resolution of issues or bugs on the site.
